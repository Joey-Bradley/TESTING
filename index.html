About

Curbside Chefs is bringing a whole lot of good food to the curb, curbing your hunger and satisfying your penchant for good taste in one fell swoop. How is this possible? Well, they're specializing in comfort food alongside those all American classics with a big menu full of bigger portions to satisfy even the biggest appetites. So hold on to your seats, there's a plethora of good eats on the streets. And it's all coming to you in a food truck roaming in Hartford.

At Curbside Chefs, you can count on their commitment to quality, relegated to using only the freshest ingredients to craft each and every meal. Craving a burger? Wake up your senses with their big ol' breakfast burger, starting with an all beef patty and piled high with a fried egg, hashbrowns and crispy bacon strips. How about the quintessential cheesesteak? Curbside Chefs' got those too, stuffed with steak hot off the grill with oh so much melted cheese. But that's not all Curbside Chefs has to offer. They've got hot dogs, chili dogs, tater tots, and even fried mac & cheese. Yes, you read that right. So why wait? Find Curbside Chefs cruising the curbs in Hartford. Trust us. It's a surefire way to satisfaction.
